# 🎬 Creative Alibi — 3-Minute Demo Video Script

**Product**: Creative Alibi: Authenticity Companion  
**Event**: AI Builders Challenge, July 2026  
**Theme**: "Reimagine Creative Industries with AI"  
**Duration**: 3:00 minutes  

---

## PRE-PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Tone**: Confident, clear, empathetic — not technical jargon
- **Audience**: Hackathon judges — some technical, some business/product
- **Key message**: "AI + trust infrastructure, not AI + evasion"
- **Must highlight**: IBM Granite, watsonx.ai, Granite Guardian
- **B-roll needed**: Word plugin UI, web dashboard, report PDF, badge

---

## SCRIPT

---

### 🎬 SCENE 1: THE HOOK — [00:00 – 00:20]

**[ON SCREEN]**: Black screen. Fade in: "Sarah is a freelance writer."

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Sarah spent 3 hours writing an 800-word article for a tech publication. Her client paid her. Then two days later — rejection. Her article was flagged by an AI detector. 91% AI probability. Sarah is devastated. She wrote every word herself. She just can't prove it."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Show a fake AI detector result showing "91% AI-Generated" on a clearly human-written piece.

**VOICEOVER**:
> "False-positive AI detection is destroying careers. And the creators who suffer most have no way to fight back."

---

### 🎬 SCENE 2: THE PROBLEM — [00:20 – 00:45]

**[ON SCREEN]**: Split screen — a creator writing on one side, an AI detector on the other. Red "FLAGGED" stamp.

**VOICEOVER**:
> "AI detectors scan the *final text*. They don't see the 3 hours of typing. The 23 revisions. The pauses to think. The deleted paragraphs. They see words — and they guess."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Text overlay: "The evidence has always been there. It just wasn't being captured."

**VOICEOVER**:
> "What genuine creators need isn't a way to fool detectors. They need a way to prove the truth."

---

### 🎬 SCENE 3: INTRODUCING CREATIVE ALIBI — [00:45 – 01:10]

**[ON SCREEN]**: Creative Alibi logo appears. Tagline: "The Trust Layer of the Creative Process."

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Creative Alibi is an Authenticity Companion for content creators. It captures the behavioral fingerprint of your writing — while you write — and turns it into verifiable evidence of your creative process."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Three icons animate in: 📊 Process Ledger | 🤖 AI Creative Partner | 📄 Authenticity Report

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Three components. The Process Ledger, the AI Creative Partner powered by IBM Granite, and the Authenticity Report. Let's see them in action."

---

### 🎬 SCENE 4: LIVE DEMO — THE WORD PLUGIN — [01:10 – 02:00]

**[ON SCREEN]**: Microsoft Word opens. The Creative Alibi ribbon button is visible. Click it. The task pane slides open.

**VOICEOVER**:
> "The Creative Alibi Add-in lives inside Microsoft Word. Click 'Start Tracking' and the Process Ledger begins — capturing your typing cadence, natural pauses, and revision patterns, locally on your device."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Writer types in Word. The tracker metrics update live in the sidebar — cadence score rises, word count increases, the Creative Timeline animates.

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Watch the Creative Timeline build in real-time. Every typing burst, every thinking pause, every revision — all captured. Cadence score: 88 out of 100. That's a natural human writing rhythm."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Writer clicks the "AI Partner" tab. Types: "How can I make this conclusion more impactful?"

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Now watch this. The writer asks IBM Granite for a style suggestion. Notice the disclosure banner — every AI interaction is logged in the Process Ledger. It's never hidden."

**[ON SCREEN]**: IBM Granite responds with hedged suggestions: "You might consider ending with a question..." The writer clicks "Accept." A log entry appears: "AI assist: style_suggestion — accepted."

**VOICEOVER**:
> "The writer accepted one suggestion. That's now disclosed in the report — transparently. IBM Granite helped. The writer decided. That's the difference."

---

### 🎬 SCENE 5: GENERATING THE REPORT — [02:00 – 02:30]

**[ON SCREEN]**: Writer clicks the "Report" tab. Eligibility checks show all green. Click "Generate Authenticity Report."

**VOICEOVER**:
> "After writing, one click generates the Authenticity Report. IBM Granite writes the narrative summary. The system calls a third-party AI detector — exactly once — to add a transparency signal."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Report generates. Status: "ISSUED ✓". Shows: Composite Score 91/100, cadence 88, paste ratio 4.2%, 23 revisions, AI assist disclosed.

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Report issued. Composite score: 91 out of 100. The AI detector gave a 12% AI probability — but that's an estimate, not a verdict. The Process Ledger is the real evidence."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Writer downloads the PDF. The Verified Process Badge appears.

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Sarah attaches this report to her next submission. Her creative process is now documented, signed, and shareable."

---

### 🎬 SCENE 6: THE WEB DASHBOARD — [02:30 – 02:50]

**[ON SCREEN]**: Switch to the Creative Alibi web dashboard. Show the overview stats: 21 reports issued, 83 average cadence score.

**VOICEOVER**:
> "The web dashboard gives a complete view — session history, deep analytics, all reports, and a full-screen AI Creative Partner. Every chart is powered by real behavioral data."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Quick scroll through Analytics page showing cadence chart, pause distribution, authorship radar.

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Writing behavior, visualized. The pause distribution, the revision map, the typing rhythm over time. This is the data that proves genuine authorship — and it's been there all along."

---

### 🎬 SCENE 7: CLOSE — [02:50 – 03:00]

**[ON SCREEN]**: Final card. Creative Alibi logo. Tagline: "Prove you wrote it. Protect what's yours."

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Creative Alibi doesn't fool detectors. It makes real creative work defensible, transparent, and portable. Built for honest creators. Powered by IBM Granite."

**[ON SCREEN]**: Text animates in:
> ✓ Process Ledger — Behavioral fingerprint of authentic authorship  
> ✓ IBM Granite — AI assistance, fully disclosed  
> ✓ Authenticity Report — Shareable, verifiable, tamper-evident  

**VOICEOVER**:
> "Creative Alibi. Built with IBM Granite for the AI Builders Challenge."

**[ON SCREEN]**: IBM logo + watsonx.ai badge + Creative Alibi logo. Fade to black.
