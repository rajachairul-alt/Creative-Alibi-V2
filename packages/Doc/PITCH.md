# 🎤 Creative Alibi — 3-Minute Pitch Script

**Event**: AI Builders Challenge, July 2026  
**Theme**: "Reimagine Creative Industries with AI"  
**Format**: 3-minute spoken pitch (for judges panel or demo video voiceover)  

---

## TIMING GUIDE

| Section | Duration | Lines |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Hook + Problem | 0:45 | Paragraph 1–3 |
| Solution | 0:45 | Paragraph 4–5 |
| Product Demo (3 features) | 1:00 | Paragraph 6–8 |
| IBM Granite highlight | 0:20 | Paragraph 9 |
| Impact + Close | 0:10 | Paragraph 10 |

---

## SCRIPT

*[Speak clearly. Pause after each major point.]*

---

**[0:00 — HOOK]**

> "Imagine you're a freelance writer. You spend three hours writing an article, send it to your client — and get rejected. Not because the work was bad. Because a detector flagged it as 91% AI-generated. You wrote every word. But you can't prove it."

*[Pause 2 seconds]*

---

**[0:15 — THE SCALE OF THE PROBLEM]**

> "This is happening to writers, students, and journalists every day. AI content detectors are now used by universities, publishers, and clients to screen submissions — and they produce false positives at alarming rates. The problem isn't that creators are using AI to cheat. The problem is that honest creators have no defense when they're wrongly accused."

---

**[0:35 — THE MISSING PIECE]**

> "What's been missing isn't better detection. It's the other side of the equation: verifiable proof of genuine authorship. The behavioral fingerprint of real writing — the pauses, the backspacing, the rhythm of thought-to-word. That evidence has always existed. It just hasn't been captured. Until now."

---

**[0:55 — INTRODUCING CREATIVE ALIBI]**

> "This is Creative Alibi: Authenticity Companion. The trust layer of the creative process."

*[Show product logo]*

> "Creative Alibi has three core components. The Process Ledger — a local behavioral record of how you write. The AI Creative Partner — powered by IBM Granite. And the Authenticity Report — a cryptographically-signed document you attach to any submission."

---

**[1:20 — FEATURE 1: PROCESS LEDGER]**

> "When you write in Microsoft Word with our Add-in installed, the Process Ledger runs silently in the background. It captures your typing cadence, your natural thinking pauses, your revision patterns. Not the text — the behavior. A cadence score of 88 out of 100 means your typing rhythm looks like a real human writer's. A copy-paste ratio of 4% means almost nothing was pasted in. These aren't approximations — they're measurements."

---

**[1:50 — FEATURE 2: IBM GRANITE AI CREATIVE PARTNER]**

> "Now — and this is what makes Creative Alibi different from any other solution — we embrace AI assistance, openly."

*[Pause]*

> "If a writer wants help with their phrasing, they can ask IBM Granite. But every suggestion is logged, timestamped, and tagged as AI-assisted. The writer explicitly accepts or declines. Nothing is ever silently merged into their work. When a judge reads their Authenticity Report and sees '2 IBM Granite suggestions, 1 accepted, 1 declined' — that's transparency. That's trust."

---

**[2:20 — FEATURE 3: AUTHENTICITY REPORT]**

> "After a session, one click generates the Authenticity Report. IBM Granite writes a factual narrative summary. A third-party AI detector runs exactly once — just to add a transparency signal. And the entire Process Ledger is compiled into a PDF the writer can attach to any submission. It's not a guarantee. It's defensible supporting evidence. And it's the best evidence that's ever existed for genuine authorship."

---

**[2:45 — IBM GRANITE HIGHLIGHT]**

> "IBM Granite powers both the AI Creative Partner and the report narrative. We use the `granite-3-3b-instruct` model via watsonx.ai — chosen for its instruction-following precision and hackathon-friendly latency. Granite Guardian validates every output before it reaches the writer. IBM's technology isn't just integrated — it's essential to what makes this trustworthy."

---

**[2:55 — CLOSE]**

> "Creative Alibi doesn't fool detectors. It makes real creative work defensible, transparent, and portable. Built for honest creators. Powered by IBM Granite. Thank you."

---

## DELIVERY NOTES

- **Slow down on**: "The Process Ledger runs silently..." (technical concept, let it land)
- **Emphasize**: "Nothing is ever silently merged" — this is the key differentiator
- **Tone shift**: More excited at "one click generates..." — this is the payoff
- **Eye contact**: Look up from notes at "This is Creative Alibi" and at the close
- **Avoid**: Saying "AI-proof" or "detector-beating" — this product is explicitly not that
- **Q&A prep**: "What if someone games the tracker?" → "It's supporting evidence, not a guarantee — the report is transparent about its own limits"
